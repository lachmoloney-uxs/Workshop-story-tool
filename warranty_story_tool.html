How to use this tool
Enter your RO number, select the job type, describe the fault in your own words and tap Generate. The AI writes a clean 3 C's story. Copy and paste into Eclipse on the correct job.
What are the 3 C's?
CONCERN: What the customer said was wrong. CAUSE: What you found when you looked at it. CORRECTION: What was done to fix it.
Tips for describing the fault
You do not need perfect English. Just tell the tool what happened and it will clean it up.
customer say noise from front when turn left. found worn CV joint. replaced CV joint and boot.
engine light on. scan show misfire cylinder 3. replace ignition coil cylinder 3. light clear.
customer say AC not cold. found gas low and leak at condenser. replace condenser and regas.
Multiple stories per RO
Each time you generate for the same RO a new version is saved automatically. Tap Load or tap the RO in the saved list to see all versions and choose which one to load or delete.
Voice input
Tap Speak and talk normally. Tap Stop when finished. Go to Settings to change the voice language if transcription is not accurate.
After generating
Use the green button to copy the full story, or Copy on each section individually. Paste into Eclipse, tick your cover sheet, hand to the Foreman.
