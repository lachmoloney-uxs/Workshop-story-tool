How to use this tool
Enter your RO number at the top, then describe the fault in your own words. The AI will write a clean warranty story in the 3 C's format. Copy it and paste into Eclipse on the correct job.
What are the 3 C's?
Every warranty story needs three parts:
CONCERN: What the customer said was wrong with the car. CAUSE: What you found when you looked at it. CORRECTION: What was done to fix it.
Tips for describing the fault
You do not need perfect English. Just tell the tool what happened in your own words and it will clean it up for you.
Good examples:
customer say noise from front when turn left. found worn CV joint. replaced CV joint and boot.
engine light on. scan show misfire cylinder 3. replace ignition coil cylinder 3. light clear.
customer say AC not cold. found AC gas low and leak at condenser. replace condenser and regas system.
Using voice input
Tap Speak and talk normally. Tap Stop when finished. The tool transcribes what you said — you can edit it before generating. Go to Settings to change the voice language if transcription is not accurate.
Saving and loading stories
Stories are automatically saved against the RO number. To reload a story, enter the RO number and tap Load, or tap any story in the saved list.
After generating
Use the green button to copy the full story, or use the Copy button on each section individually. Paste into Eclipse, tick your cover sheet, and hand to the Foreman.
